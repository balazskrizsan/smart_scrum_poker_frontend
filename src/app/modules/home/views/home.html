Smarter Estimations for Agile Teams

Traditional planning poker relies on Fibonacci numbers, but software development is rarely that simple. Our Scrum Poker approach redefines estimation by focusing on what truly matters: Uncertainty, Complexity, and Effort.

Instead of arbitrary numbers, team members assign tasks based on three clear categories

With just three intuitive options—Small, Medium, and Big—teams can quickly align on estimates, reduce bias, and improve planning accuracy. Say goodbye to misleading numbers and hello to meaningful discussions!

  • Uncertainty – How well do we understand the task?

  • Complexity – How intricate is the implementation?

  • Effort – How much work is required?
